Queue Up

Who are we?

We are the blue men. Our names are Aaron Chen, Christopher Tian, Kenny Wang, and Steven Yen. We created Queue Up because many times we have found that we are playing League of Legends or Counter Strike: Global Offensive alone. We all love to play these games however we would rather prefer to play with somebody else. In order to fulfill this need, we thought up of Queue Up so no one has to endure the pain of a solo queue again. Thank you for visiting us and we hope you enjoy our product.
